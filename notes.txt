Show me the top 5 days with highest protein intake

SELECT day, sum(protein_g) AS total_protein_g FROM servings_apr_25 GROUP BY day ORDER BY total_protein_g DESC LIMIT 5

Which day was the most sugar eaten, and how much? Round to the nearest integer. Output in the format "DATE AMOUNT", for example: "2021-03-15 99g"

SELECT 
		day, 
		sum(coalesce(sugars_g, 0)) as total_sugar
FROM servings_apr_25
GROUP BY day
ORDER BY total_sugar DESC
LIMIT 1
